# Monetization Language Fix - Viewer-Focused ✅

## 🎯 **Issue Fixed**

The previous language ("Earn Money") was confusing because:
- **From creator's perspective**: They ARE earning money (correct)
- **From viewer's perspective**: They're DONATING/SUPPORTING, not earning (confusing!)

## ✅ **Changes Made**

### 1. **Badge Updated**
- ❌ **Before**: "💰 Earn Money"
- ✅ **After**: "💝 Support"
- **Icon changed**: Dollar sign → Heart icon (more friendly)

### 2. **Text Indicator Updated**
- ❌ **Before**: "💰 Earn Money"
- ✅ **After**: "💝 Support Creator"
- **Clearer message**: Viewers understand they're supporting, not earning

### 3. **Tooltip Descriptions Updated**
All tooltips now focus on **supporting the creator**:

- **Ko-fi**: "Support the creator! Buy them a coffee or purchase digital products."
- **Patreon**: "Support the creator with monthly subscriptions and get exclusive benefits."
- **PayPal**: "Send money directly to support the creator quickly and easily."
- **Gumroad**: "Purchase digital products like guides, presets, or exclusive content."
- **GoFundMe**: "Support the creator's projects, goals, or help in times of need."
- **Stripe**: "Purchase products or services from the creator."

### 4. **Code Comments Updated**
- Updated internal comments to reflect "support/donations" instead of "earn money"

---

## 🎨 **Visual Changes**

### Badge:
- **Icon**: 💝 Heart (was 💰 dollar sign)

- **Color**: Still yellow/gold (friendly, welcoming)

### Text Indicator:
- **Text**: "💝 Support Creator" (was "💰 Earn Money")
- **Clear call-to-action**: Viewers know they're supporting
